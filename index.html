About the Game

The three categories of the Hunter 2021 Codefest are Productivity, Social Good, and Education! As such we wanted to make something fun, interactive, and educational. This game and website is intended to inform people about the importance of the actions we take during a pandemic. The weight of each action has a cost and benefit. Through the course of the day, weeks, and months all these actions and their effects all add up.

In this game, you start off as player living in your own home in the beginning of a pandemic. Through the process of the game, you must balance various factors like work, entertainment, and finances to hopefully lead a life of your choosing!
